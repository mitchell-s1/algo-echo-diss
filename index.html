The Psychological Impact of Algorithmic Echo Chambers

Identity, Curiosity, Moral Intuition & Psychopathology in Digital Environments

Explore Project

Project Overview

Algorithmic feeds—optimized for engagement, not truth—create chronic epistemic instability. My dissertation integrates depth-psychology, cognitive science and clinical theory to test whether these environments trigger regressive restoration of the self: a retreat to rigid, absolutist meaning structures that regulate distress at the cost of flexibility and reality-testing.

Cognitive

Deprivation curiosity & illusory-truth loops.

Moral

Identity-protective cognition & moralization.

Clinical

Amplification of schizotypy, paranoia, thought disorder.

Integrated Ecological Model

Algorithmic reinforcement → Epistemic destabilization → Identity fusion / symptom amplification
